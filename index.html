StandOrDie Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 12, 2026

Overview

StandOrDie is a sit/stand reminder app. Your privacy is simple: we don't collect, store, or share any of your data. Everything stays on your device.

Data Collection

StandOrDie does not collect any personal information. The app does not require an account, email address, or any form of registration.

Sensors

The app uses your device's accelerometer and pedometer to detect movement. This sensor data is processed entirely on your device in real time and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted.

Analytics

The app tracks your daily sitting and standing time to display in-app statistics. This data is stored locally on your device using on-device storage and is never sent to any server.

Third-Party Services

StandOrDie does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Network

StandOrDie does not connect to the internet. The app functions entirely offline.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL.
